The dataset contains observational data of 201 patient from 4 groups: 
- 1- HIV-positive with tuberculous meningitis (HIV pos - TBM)
- 2- HIV-positive with cryptococcal meningitis (HIV pos - CM)
- 3- HIV neg - TBM 
- 4- HIV neg - CM


Variables:
- code      - Patient identifier
- hiv       - 0=HIV negative, 1=HIV positive
- diagnosis - CM or TBM
- group     - 1 to 4 (see above)
- age
- sex       - 1=male, 2=female
- groupLong - Longer group description
- bldwcc    - White cell count in blood (10^3/mm3)
- bldneut   - Neutrophil count in blood
- bldlym    - Lymphocyte count in blood
- csfwcc    - White cell count in CSF
- csfneut   - Neutrophil count in CSF
- csflym    - Lymphocyte count in CSF
